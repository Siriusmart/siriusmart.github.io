Climate zones

Name Where Features
Tropical climate Near the equator Heavy rainfall and thunderstorm (rainforests)
Sub-tropical (desert) climate 30 degrees north and south Dry weather with no cloud cover
Temperate climate 50 - 60 degrees north and south Clouds and frequent rainfall
Polar climate At the poles Dry icy winds caused by the spin of the Earth

Monsoons

Spring/Summer

  1. Land heats up faster than ocean
  2. Hot air on land rises creating low pressure
  3. Rainfall on land and surface wind blows from ocean to land

Autumn/Winter

  1. Land cools down faster than ocean
  2. Hot air on ocean rises creating low pressure
  3. Rainfall on ocean and surface wind blows from land to ocean
