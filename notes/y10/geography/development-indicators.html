Development Indicators

AC (Advanced countries)

  • Well developed financial markets
  • High degrees of financial intermediation (borrowing/lending money)
  • Diversified economy structure
  • Rapaidly growing service sectors

EDC (Emerging developing counties)

  • Does not share all characteristic of ACs
  • Not elegible for the Proverty Reduction and Growth Act

LIDC (Low income developing countries)

  • Elegible for the Proverty Reduction and Growth Act

Indicators

Type Description
Social To do with quality of life of individuals in that country
Economic To do with wealth of individuals in that country

HDI - Human development index

  • Measures life expectency, level of education and income
  • Score between 0 and 1 (higher is better)

GII - Gender inequality indicator

  • Reproductive health - Mortality ratio and adolecent birth rates
  • Empowerment - Proportion of political seats occupied by females
  • Female older than 25 years old with secondary education

GNI - Gross national income (per capita)

  • Income per person
