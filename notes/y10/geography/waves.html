Waves

Wave energy

Wave energy depends on the fetch

Fetch

Distance over which the wave has travelled

Longer fetch = more energy (because there is more time for the wave to gain energy)

  1. Each wave has a circular motion of water
  2. When it gets close to shore friction with seabed casuses the motion to be elliptical
  3. Top of wave moves faster than bottom because of less friction
  4. The wave breaks
  5. Water washes up the beach as the previous wave comes down

Constructive waves

  • Waves that pushes material up the beach
  • Shorter in height
  • More foward energy
  • Longer fetch

Destructive waves

  • Waves that pulls material down the beach
  • Taller in height
  • Less forward energy more downward energy
  • Shorter fetch
