The Rostow Model

Stages of development

1. The traditional society

  • Primary industries
  • Economy vulerable to uncontrollable influences (e.g.Â disease, war)

2. Pre-conditions for take off

  • Begin to experience globalization
  • Secondary industries starts to take off
  • Begin to have surplus produce (so they can trade)

3. Take-off

  • Secondary industries dominates the economy
  • Industrialization

4. Drive to maturity

  • More involved in tertiary jobs
  • Universities and easy school access
  • Self-reliance
  • Urbanization

5. High mass consumption

  • Tertiary jobs dominate the economy
  • Factories with less environmental impact
