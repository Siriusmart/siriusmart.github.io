Drainage basins

  • An area with flowing rivers surrounded by mountains
  • Any water in the drainage basin will find its way into the rivers and flows out of the area
Name Description
Watershed The highland separating the drainage basins
Source Where rivers starts
Tributary A smaller river stream that joins the main area
Confluence Where 2 rivers meet
Mouth Where rivers flows into the sea
Estuary Where the mouth is low enough for the sea to enter

Long profile of rivers

The long profile river has a high altitude, dropping altitude quickily, the drop then slows down.

Course River cross section shape
Upper V shaped very steep
Middle V shaped but wider
Lower Very wide and flat, water flows in slow speed
