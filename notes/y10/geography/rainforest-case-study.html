Rainforest Case Study

Nutrient cycle

  • Rapaid nutrient cycle
  • Very few litter because hot and damp forest floor make leaves decompose quickly
  • Large biomass
  • Shallow soil/infertile top soil (because nutrients are taken from the soil)

Indigeonous people

  • Home to 50M people
  • Hunter-gatherers
  • Shifting cultivation (ash from burt down trees make soil fertile, leaves after a growing season to prevent permanent damage)

Goods from tropical rainforests

  • Medicine raw materials
  • Fibres
  • Flavorings
  • Gum and resin
  • Fruits and vegatables
  • Wood
  • Rubber

Importance of rainforests

  • Trees reduce flood risk (leaves intercepts water)
  • Maintain soil and protect from erosion
  • Habitat for wide range of wildlife
  • Carbon sink
  • Maintain water cycle
