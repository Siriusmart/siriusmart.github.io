Goat aid

What the programme is

  • UK NGO loans 3 goats to poor households headed by women
  • Women are provided with a training package to teach them how to take care and breed goats
  • To repay the loan women need to provided 3 goat kids
  • Those goats can then be given to other women

Why it should improve development

  • Families can then use the money from selling goats to send children to school
  • Goat owning families are becoming healthier
