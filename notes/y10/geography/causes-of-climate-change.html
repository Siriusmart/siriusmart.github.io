Causes of Climate Change

1. Milankovitch cycles (Natural)

  • Eccentricity: The Earth’s orbit
  • Axial tilt: The earth’s titing axis
  • Precession: Natural wobble that occurs on Earth

2. Volcabuc eruptions (Natural)

  • Volcanic eruptions blasts huge quantity of ash
  • Ash reflects sunlight off, with a cooling effect

3. Sunspot activity (Natural)

Definition

Sunspots are dark patches on the sun’s surface

  • Sunspots are associated with strong magnetic fields
  • When there are more sunspots, the sun gives out more heat

4. Enhanced Greenhouse effect (Human)

Greenhouse gases traps infrared heat in the atmosphere

Main greenhouse gases

  • Carbon dioxide
  • Methane
  • Nitrous oxide
