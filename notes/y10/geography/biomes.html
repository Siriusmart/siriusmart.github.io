Biomes

Tropical grasslands (Savanna)

  • Location: Mid Africa, east of South America, north Australia (Mostly southern hemisphere)
  • Seasonal rain
  • Around 25 degrees Celcius yearround

Animal adaptations

  • Baobab (tree): thick trunks stores water during dry seasons, bark is fireproof
  • Lion: Fur color camouflage, runs up to 40 mph

Temperate grasslands

  • Location: 30 - 50 degrees
  • Rain all year round

Temperate forests

  • Location: Between 40 - 60 degrees
  • 5 degrees Celcius in January and 15 degrees Celcius in July

Animal adaptations

  • Fox: Front facing ears locates prey, fur color camouflage
  • Some animals migrate to warmer places in winter

Hot deserts

  • Location: 30 degrees (high pressure)
  • Very low precipitation
  • Plants adapts to high salt condition

Tundras

  • Location: Northern Russia, Canada and Greenland
  • -10 degrees Celcius in January and 8 degrees Celcius in July
  • Rainfall year round (150mm)
