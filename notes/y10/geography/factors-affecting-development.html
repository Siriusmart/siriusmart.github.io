Factors Affecting Development

Population

  • Larger population means more working force

Climate

  • Too hot: More disease
  • Temperate: Warm, moist climate is good for growing crops

Land

  • Flat fertile land: Better crop grow + easier to develop
  • Coastal countries: Easy trade

Natural resources

  • Resources like oil or coal help kickstart development
  • Access to safe drinking water
  • Precious metals
