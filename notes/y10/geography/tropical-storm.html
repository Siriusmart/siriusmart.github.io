Tropical Storm

  • A low pressure system
  • At tropical oceans
  • Circular storm with high wind speed and heavy rain

Frequency

  • There are around 80 stroms per year
  • Most of them are in the east Pacific
  • There is no obvious relations between global warming and the frequency of storms

Case study: Haiyan

Location: Philippines

Time: 2013

Impacts

  • Storm surge of 5m
  • Waves of 15m

Made worse by large population at vulnerable coastal areas
