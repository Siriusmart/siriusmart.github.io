Note

  • Electrical current only go in the direction of the arrow in a diode (it also goes in one direction in an LED)
  • The longer side of the battery/cell is the positive side of it

Circuit Rules

  1. Sum of current entering a junction is equal to the sum of current leaving it
  2. Voltage in series att up together
  3. The higher the resistance, the less current pass through the branch (V = IR)
  4. The voltage used in the 2 branches should be the same, the current may differ according to the resistance
