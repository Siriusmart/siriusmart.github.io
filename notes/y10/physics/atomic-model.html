Atomic Model

  • No. of protons: Determin what an atom is
  • No. of electrons: Determin reactivity (chemistry)
  • No. of protons ≠ No. of electrons: An ion (with charge)

History

400BC

Greek philosopher Democrites:

  • All matter was made up of small indivisible particles (ATOMA)

1804

World reowned chemist John Dalton:

  • All matters was made up of small, indivisible spheres (Atoms)

1897

Plum pudding model by J.J. Tompson:

Particle Description
Electrons Small negatively charged particles
Pudding Positive sphere of matter
Plum Electrons

1901

Gold foil experiement by Rutherford:

Fire alpha particles at a sheet of thin gold foil

Results

  • Most alpha particles travelled straight through the foil
  • 1 in 2000 were deflected in angles smaller than 90 degrees
  • 1 in 8000 were bounced back

Conclusion

  • Mass of atom is concentrated and positively charged (as only small amount of alpha particles were repelled)
