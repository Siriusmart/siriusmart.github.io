Newton’s Laws of Motion

Newton’s First Law

  • If the forces are balanced, the velocity of the object will not change (0 acceleration)
  • If the forces acting on is are imbalanced, the object will accelerate or change direction

Note

When changing direction the velocity is constantly changing but the speed remains the same

Newton’s Second Law

  • The larger the force, the greater the acceleration
  • Acceleration is proportional to the force
  • F = ma

Newton’s Third Law

  • Each force has a reaction force of oppsite direction but equal in force

Note

  • The floor is pushing you up same as you are pressing it down
  • You are attracted by the Earth’s gravity same as the Earth is attracted by your gravity
