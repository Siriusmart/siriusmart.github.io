Uses of Radioactivity

Food irradiation

  • Kills bacteria and other pathogens
  • Does not make food radioactive
  • Does not get rid of toxins

Chain reaction

  1. Requires the uranium 235 isotope
  2. One neutron to start the chain reaction
  3. The uranium 235 then decays into a bunch of other unstable atoms, releasing 2 neutrons in the process
  4. The 2 neutrons is then used to make other uranium 235 decay
  5. When it decays it loses some mass, it is loss as energy
  6. The energy is then used for whatever perpose
