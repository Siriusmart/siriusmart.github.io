Earthing

  • Connected to the metal case in one side, the ground the other side
  • In case of exposed wire touching metal casing, the current will flow through the earth wire instead of the idiot trying to get himself killed because the earth wire has less resistance

Earthing symbol

Double insulated

  • Basically means no matter how much exposed wire there is, the current will stay inside (the appliances are also waterproof)
  • No earthing needed

Fuse

  • When too much current it heats up and melts, breaking the circuit
  • Usually want to have a fuse that will blow only when current is slightly higher than the normal usage

MCB (Miniature Circuit Breaker)

  • When too much current it breaks

RCCB (Residual Current Circuit Breaker)

  • Compares if there is a difference in current between the live and neutral wire
  • If there is a difference there is likely that the current are going elsewhere can RCCB will break the circuit
