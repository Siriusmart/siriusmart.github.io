Tendency of stability

Stuff usually try to get rid of excess energy, if possible.

Strong nuclear force

  • Very strong force responsible for holding the necleons together.
  • Have a very short range (only works in the nucleus because of its range)
  • All necleons are attracted to each other by this force.
Particle Description
Neutron Gives strong nuclear force (acts like glue)
Proton Gives strong nuclear force but also repels each other at the same time

Note

When the protons are too far apart, they are out of the range of the nuclear force and therefore only repel each other and destabilize the atom.
