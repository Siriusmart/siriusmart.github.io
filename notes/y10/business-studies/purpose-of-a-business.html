Purpose of a Business

Enterprise

  • Another name for a “business”
  • The actions of someone who takes a risk by setting up, investing in and running a business

Entrepreneur

  • Someone who takes a calculated risk through starting a business

Entrepreneurs

Characteristics

  • Tries to exploit a business opportunity
  • Take time to understand and calculate the risks involved
  • Make an investment to set up the business (often their own money)
  • They go ahead dispite the risk that they might fail

Objectives

  • To be their own boss/make their own decisions
  • To keep their own profits
  • Don’t have a job/not satisfied with their current job
  • To provide a service
  • To prove something themselves
