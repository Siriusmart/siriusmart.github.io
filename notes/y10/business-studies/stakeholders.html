Stakeholders

Definition

A stakeholder is any individual or organisation who has an interest in the activities and decision making of a business

  • An individual or group with a direct interest
  • Main stakeholders: Employees, shareholders, customers, suppliers, the local community and financiers
  • Stakeholders have no formal authority over the business but it’s best for the business’ long term interests to pay close attention to their needs
Stakeholder Interest
Shareholders Share price
Employees Security, income, working conditions
Customers Price, safely, certainty of supply
Suppliers Price, prompt payment, regular orders
Local community Employmennt, environment
Internal stakeholder External stakeholder
A member of the organisation Not part of the organisation
