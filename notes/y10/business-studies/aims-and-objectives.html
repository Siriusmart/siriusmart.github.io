Aims and Objectives

Aims Objectives
Long term strategic targets Goals or targets a business tries to achieve to meet its aims
Give a sense of direction
  1. [Aim] States overall purpose of the business (long term goal)
  2. [Mission statement] General description of overall aims
  3. Objectives Specific, measurable targets

Objectives

Smart objectives

  • Specific
  • Measurable
  • Achieveable
  • Realistic
  • Timed

Common business objectives

  • Survival
  • Profit maximasation
  • Market share
  • Growth

Considerations

  • Social/ethical responsibility
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Shareholder value

Reasons of setting objectives

  • To provide direction
  • All employees follow the same objective
  • Allow for planning
  • Measurement of success

Factors determine the business objectives

  • Size of business
  • Level of competition
  • Type of business

Changing objectives

Internal reasons External reasons
One objective achieved New competitor
Change of view Change in economic environment
Required by shareholders Technological change

Company makes enough profit to satisfy its shareholders

Ethical Objectives

  • Aim to “do the right thing”
  • Insurance against bad publicity
  • Generate more sales
  • Useful when marketing
