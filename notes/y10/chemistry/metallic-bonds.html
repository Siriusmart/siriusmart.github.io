Metallic Bonds

  1. When metal atoms come together, electrons can leave their outer shell
  2. Delocalized electrons move freely forming a sea of electrons
  3. Metal ions are attracted to the sea of electrons

Diagram

Properties

  • Malleable, because all the atoms are at same size so they can slide on each other easily
  • Conducts electricity, because delocalized electrons are free to move around and carry charge
