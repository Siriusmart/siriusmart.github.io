Electroplate

Electroplating is a type of electrolysis that uses non-inert electrodes

Process

  • Anode: Pure silver or whatever you want it to be electroplated in (if it forms negetive ions then cathode)
  • The other electrode: Whatever you want to electroplate (must be conductive)
  • Electrolyte: Something containing the thing you want to electroplate (e.g.Â if silver then the elctrolyte can be silver nitrate)
