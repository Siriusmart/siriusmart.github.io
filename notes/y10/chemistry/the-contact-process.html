The Contact Process

Sulfuric acid is used to make fertilisers

Raw materials

  • Sulphur
  • Air (for oxygen)
  • Water

1. Sulfur -> Sulfur dioxide

Sulfur burns in air

S(s) + O₂(g) -> SO₂(g)

-297kJ/mol

2. Contact process

Sulfur dioxide -> Sulfur trioxide

Formula

SO₂(g) + O₂(g) <=> 2SO₃(g)

-144kJ/mol

Conditions

  • 2 atmospheric pressures (higher pressure favors side with less moles)
  • 450 degrees Celsius
  • Catalyst V₂O₅(aq)

96% yield

3. Sulfur trioxide -> Sulfuric acid

H₂O(l) + SO₃(g) -> H₂SO₄(aq)
