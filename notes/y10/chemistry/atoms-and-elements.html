Atoms and Elements

Atoms

  • Smallest particles of an element that has its own chemical properties
  • Is neutral in charge
  • Protons and neutrons in the nucleus, electrons surrounding the nucleus in shells

Properties of subatomic particles

Particle Mass (Relative) Charge (Relative)
Proton 1 +1
Neutron 1 0
Electron 0.0005 -1

Chemical Symbol

  • Each atom has its chemical symbol
  • Atomic number (number of protons, lower)
  • Mass number (relative mass of atom, upper)

Copper Chemical Symbol

Molecules

Made from 2 or more atoms bonded together.

Mixtures

2 or more elements mixed together, and not chemically bonded together.

Elements in the mixtures can usually easily be separated.

Isotopes

An atom with different number of neutrons.

Units

Aᵣ: Relative atomic mass (defined by a carbon atom is exactly 12, so hydrogen is around 1) Mᵣ: Relative molecular mass
