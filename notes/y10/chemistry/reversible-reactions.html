Reversible reactions

Reactants can be turned into products, and products can be turned back into reactants.

Dynamic equilibrium

  • When the foward reaction is the same speed as the backward reaction, system is in dynamic equilibrium.
  • Has to be a closed system
  • The amount of reactants and products do not change when at dynamic equilibrium.

Change in conditions

Note

The opposite is true * Increase in temperature: favours the endothermic reaction. * Increase in pressure: favours the side with less moles (more of the side with less moles). * Increase in reactants: turns more reactants into products.
