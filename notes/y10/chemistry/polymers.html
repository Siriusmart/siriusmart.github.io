Polymers

Polypers are many monomers chained together end to end.

Examples

  • Ethene molecules join end to end to make polythene (poly-ethene)
  • Amino acid molecules join end to end to make a protein

Diagram

Types of polymers

  1. Thermosoftening polymer
    • Polymers chains not connected to each other
    • Becomes soft when heated
  2. Thermosetting polymer
    • Polymers chains connected to each other with covabent bonds (cross linking)
    • Cannot be remoulded, chars and burns when heated
