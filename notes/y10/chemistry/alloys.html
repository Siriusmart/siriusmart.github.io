Alloys

Alloys are mixture of 2 or more elements (at least one of them are metal)

Properties

Note

Properties vary depending on the alloy

  • Non-malleable, because atoms are at different sizes and cannot slide on each other easily
  • Tensile strength and ductile (steel)
  • Resist corrosion (Brass and bronze)
  • Different melting points (solder melts at a low temperature of 227 degrees C, it can be used to join copper pipes)
