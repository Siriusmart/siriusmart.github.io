Redox Reactions

Definition

A process where reductions and oxidation happens at the same time

Example

2Al(s) + Fe₂O₃(s) -> Al₂O₃(s) + 2Fe(l)

  • Alumininum is oxidized (gains oxygen)
  • Fe₂O₃ is reduced (loses oxygen)

Oxidation is loss and reduction is gain (oil rig)

Note

Redox do not have to involve oxygen at all

Take this reaction as example

Zn(s) + CuCl₂(s) -> ZnCl₂(aq) + Cu(s)

  • Chloride ions are unchanged
  • Zinc atoms are oxidized to zinc ions (gain electrons)
  • Copper ions are reduced to copper atoms (lose electrons)
